I've directed SpartaHack the past two years

SpartaHack is a student-run 48 hour hackathon held at Michigan State University

  • Who Can Join?

    Anyone who is currently a student or has graduated within 12 months of SpartaHack! Graduate students, undergrads, and high-school students from any field of study are welcome.

  • Do You Need To Know how to code?

    Definitely not! No programming experience is necessary; we have workshops and mentors at the event to help you learn about a variety of topics.

Pro-Tips

  • Instead of paying for Dreamweaver, Use PHP Storm or WebStorm. It's free for students and has a ton of stuff that makes life easier. For example, instead of having to edit your changes in an FTP each time, you can set up a remote host, then Set where your files are, add the credentials in once and have it automatically upload to the server and you can view your changes with the Click of a button

  • Use SASS because dealing with CSS can be Hell sometimes. and SASS/SCSS makes it so much easier. You can nest things, use variables, and more. I don't know what I'd do without it!

  • If you don't already know, don't use Open Office. While you're a student you can get MS Office for free here! Just sign in with your MSU email, And then click install office on the upper-right hand side of the screen.
